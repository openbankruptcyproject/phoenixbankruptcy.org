Frequently Asked Questions

Which bankruptcy court do Phoenix area suburbs file in?

Most Phoenix area suburbs file in the same federal district court. Click on your city above for specific courthouse information, driving distances, and local resources.

Are Arizona exemptions the same across all Phoenix suburbs?

Arizona exemptions apply statewide, so all Phoenix area residents have the same property protections. However, the county you live in determines which federal district handles your case. View Arizona exemptions.

How do I find a bankruptcy attorney near me in the Phoenix area?

Each city page above includes local legal aid resources, bar association referral services, and law school clinics. Many attorneys offer free initial consultations. You can also check our legal aid directory.
